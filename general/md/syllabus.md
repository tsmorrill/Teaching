# Syllabus

## Grades

### Course Average

* 45% ● Assignments:
	- 10% Attendance
	- 15% Homework
	- 20% Quizzes
* 55% ★ Assignments:
	- 25% Midterm Exam
	- 30% Final Exam

### μ-Rubrics

Most problems in this course will come with a micro rubric (μ-rubric for short). The μ-rubric lists a brief partition of the points alloted for the given problem.
